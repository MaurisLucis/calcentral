Before exporting your E-Grades:

1. Select a grading scheme:

You have already set a grading scheme. You can view your grading scheme or select an alternate grading scheme in .

Set a grading scheme in and return once completed.

2. Post (publish or "unmute") all assignment grades:

To ensure that your E-Grades export matches expectations, all assignment grades must be posted. To confirm that all grades have been posted, go to your gradebook, and look for assignments with a crossed-out eye icon () indicating that an assignment has unposted grades. If you find an assignment with unposted grades, click the three vertical dot menu () , and select "post grades." You will be prompted to post grades for everyone, or only students who have been assigned a grade.

For detailed instructions, see: "How do I post grades for an assignment?"

In order to avoid errors, we suggest cross-checking final grades in the bCourses gradebook with the output CSV to confirm grades were exported as expected.

3. Set P/NP cutoff point:

The E-Grades export will automatically convert bCourses letter grades to the student-selected grading option. Please select the lowest passing letter grade.
